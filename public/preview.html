1. Overview

The Stellars is an 8-player quarterly decision-making simulation. Each player manages their company under shared economic conditions.

  • Adjust pricing, advertising, production, and sales force every quarter.
  • Compete against 7 other companies.
  • Track your company’s growth through revenue, profit, and market share.
  • View historical performance and trend graphs.

2. Decisions Sheet

Players submit quarterly decisions that affect company performance. Each round includes:

  • Price per unit
  • Advertising spend
  • Sales force size
  • Production quantity

Example:

Price: $22  
Advertising: $8,000  
Sales Force: 9 reps  
Production: 5,000 units

This tab displays competitor data, including:

  • Competitor pricing

4. Results & History

After each quarter, players receive:

  • Revenue
  • Cost of goods sold
  • Gross profit
  • Operating profit
  • Net profit
  • Quarter-to-quarter growth charts
